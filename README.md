# Historical Analysis of Average Virginia Gas Prices (1980-2021)

## Overview

This project analyzes historical gasoline prices in Virginia from 1980 through 2021 using publicly available data. The goal is to identify long-term trends, seasonal patterns, and periods of significant price volatility.

## Research Questions

This analysis seeks to answer the following questions:

1. How have average Virginia gas prices changed over time?
2. Which months tend to have the highest and lowest average prices?
3. How do average gas prices compare across decades?
4. Which years experienced the greatest price volatility?

## Data Source

The dataset contains historical average gasoline prices for Virginia from 1980 through 2021.

## Methodology

The data was organized and analyzed in Microsoft Excel.

The analysis includes:

- Trend analysis across the entire dataset
- Monthly average comparisons
- Decade-to-decade comparisons
- Identification of years with the largest price fluctuations

Charts and summary statistics were created to visualize historical patterns and support conclusions.

## Scope

This analysis examines historical Virginia gasoline prices from 1980 through 2021 using the most recent complete years available in the dataset.

## Limitations

- The dataset reflects statewide average gasoline prices and may not represent local prices in specific cities or counties.
- The analysis excludes partial-year data to maintain consistency in year-to-year comparisons.
- Results should be interpreted as historical trends and may not reflect current market conditions.
